Venue

Instructions on how visitors can get from Larnaca Airport and Paphos Airport to Limassol for BSides Cyprus 2023:

From Larnaca Airport to Limassol:

  1. Airport Shuttle: There's a convenient shuttle service from Larnaca Airport to Limassol. You can find the shuttle timetable here. Be sure to check the schedule to find a departure time that matches your arrival.
  2. Taxi or Bolt: If the shuttle schedule doesn't align with your arrival, you can always opt for a taxi or ride-sharing service like Bolt. Taxis and Bolt rides are readily available at Larnaca Airport, and the journey to Limassol takes approximately 45-60 minutes depending on traffic. The cost for a taxi or Bolt ride from Larnaca to Limassol is approximately 70 euros.
  3. Car Rental: For those who prefer flexibility during their stay, car rental services are available at Larnaca Airport. You can easily rent a car by following the signs to the car rental desks inside the terminal.

From Paphos Airport to Limassol:

  1. Airport Shuttle: There's a convenient shuttle service from Paphos Airport to Limassol. You can find the shuttle timetable here. Check the schedule in advance and choose a departure time that suits your arrival.
  2. Taxi or Bolt: If the shuttle schedule doesn't align with your arrival, taxis and Bolt rides are available at Paphos Airport. The journey to Limassol takes approximately 45-60 minutes depending on traffic. The cost for a taxi or Bolt ride from Paphos to Limassol is approximately 70 euros.
  3. Car Rental: For those who prefer the convenience of a rental car, you can rent one at Paphos Airport. Follow the signs to the car rental desks located within the airport terminal.
